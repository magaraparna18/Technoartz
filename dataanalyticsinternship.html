Internship Form

The Data Analytics internship offers a comprehensive introduction to the vital role of quality assurance in the software development lifecycle. In this program, you will learn to identify, analyze, and document defects, ensuring that applications meet high standards of quality and performance. You will gain hands-on experience with various testing methodologies, including manual and automated testing, as well as tools used for bug tracking and test management. Additionally, you will develop skills in writing test cases, conducting regression tests, and collaborating with development teams to improve software functionality. By the end of the internship, you will have a solid foundation in Data Analytics principles, equipping you to contribute effectively to real-world projects and ensure the delivery of reliable software solutions.

What we learn in the Data Analytics Internship?

Data Analytics Fundamentals:

Learn key Data Analytics tools and techniques, including Excel, SQL, and Python.

Next Level:

Advanced Data Analysis using Python, Data Visualization with Power BI and Tableau, Machine Learning, and Predictive Analytics.

Internship Objectives

Gain in-depth knowledge of Data Analytics, including how to clean, organize, and analyze large datasets.

Understand how to leverage tools like SQL, Python, and Excel for data manipulation and analytics.

Develop insights from data by creating interactive dashboards using Power BI and Tableau.

Learn how to apply statistical methods to analyze trends and patterns in data for business decision-making.

Get hands-on experience in building Machine Learning models for predictive analytics and forecasting.

Master the art of data visualization to effectively communicate data-driven insights.
